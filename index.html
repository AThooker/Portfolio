About

Out of high school I believed whole heartedly I would become an MLB baseball player after a few years playing at IU Bloomington. Contrary to my theory, I had reoccurring back issues while at IU and hung up my cleats during my collegiate sophomore season. This led me to explore different paths. I transferred to a cheaper school (Indiana University Kokomo, I switched majors (Economics -> Criminal Justice), and I ended up graduating early by taking 21 credit-semesters. I was overly eager to get out in the field and work hard at whatever was next.

I’ve been an insurance salesman, a server at a restaurant, an associate instructor for a leadership company, I took up boxing for a year or so, I tried to join the service but was declined due to my back problems, and now I am a software engineer. I have pursued jobs in various fields simply out of curiosity, uncertainty, and willingness to learn.

I love to travel. I have been all the way around the world with my fiance. Our most recent trip was to Chamonix, France and Geneva, Switzerland. I have a passion for traveling and hope to continue to do so for a long time. I am excited to be in the tech field, and look forward to where it will take me. I am a lifelong learner in a field that’s always adapting. I’m not positive where it will take me but I am excited to find out
